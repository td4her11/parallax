Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Parallax

Does Parallax make decisions for me?

No. Parallax reveals patterns in YOUR decision-making. You still make all decisions. We just show you what you've been blind to - like hiring 3 people with identical profiles who all failed. The AI catches patterns you literally can't see yourself.

How is this different from AI advice tools?

We don't give advice. We're a mirror for your thinking. Other tools tell you what to do. Parallax shows you what you're actually doing vs what you think you're doing. It's pattern recognition, not recommendations.

How many decisions do I need to log before seeing patterns?

You'll get insights from your first decision, but patterns emerge after 5-10 decisions. The more you log, the smarter the AI becomes at spotting your cognitive blind spots. Most users see their first major "aha moment" within the first week.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your decisions are encrypted, never shared, and only accessible by you. We use bank-level security (256-bit encryption). Your data is never used to train AI models or shared with third parties. You can delete everything at any time.

What happens after my 14-day free trial?

Your card is saved at signup but NOT charged during the trial. You have full access for 14 days. Cancel anytime with one click from your dashboard - if you cancel before day 14, you're never charged a cent. If you love it and don't cancel, you'll be charged $49/month (Founder) or $99/month (Pro) on day 15. We'll email you 3 days before to remind you.

How do I cancel my trial or subscription?

Click "Manage Billing" or "Cancel Trial" on your dashboard. One click, no questions asked, instant cancellation. If you cancel during the trial (days 1-14), your card is never charged. If you cancel after becoming a paying customer, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Can I use this for my team?

Yes! Pro tier includes team features. Share decisions with co-founders, get collective pattern insights, see team blind spots. Perfect for leadership teams making high-stakes decisions together.

What if I don't like it?

Click "Cancel Trial" on your dashboard during days 1-14 and you'll never be charged. Zero risk. After trial, cancel anytime from your billing portal - you keep access until the end of your paid period. We don't do dark patterns or make cancellation hard. One click, done.
